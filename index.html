May 7, 2025

Retaliation to Indian Aggression

In response to India's aggression during Operation Sindoor, the Pakistan Air Force (PAF), successfully shot down six Indian aircraft without crossing into Indian airspace. Utilizing J-10C fighter jets armed with long-range BVR (Beyond Visual Range) PL-15 missiles, the PAF engaged targets from within Pakistani airspace as Indian jets conducted their strike missions from across the border. The aircraft shot down included two Su-30MKIs, three Rafale jets, and one Mirage 2000H, all neutralized mid-air while attempting standoff attacks, highlighting the precision and deterrent strength of Pakistan's modern air defense and BVR combat capabilities.
